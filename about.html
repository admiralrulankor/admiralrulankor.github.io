About me

Hi there! I’m Gibran Alfaro, but you might know me better as Admiral Rulankor—a name that’s become my creative alias and online persona. I’m a web developer, comic creator, and an avid storyteller with a passion for building worlds, both digitally and imaginatively.

What I Do

By day, I’m crafting websites using static site generators like Jekyll and Hugo, making the web a more functional and visually appealing place. By night, I’m diving into the world of comics, weaving stories, and creating characters that come to life on the page.

I’m also proud to have written a novella as part of my school tasks and to have been the top scorer in my junior high school’s National Exams. These accomplishments remind me that hard work and curiosity can take you far—whether it’s in academics, writing, or tech.

My Interests

When I’m not coding or drawing, you’ll find me:

  • Reading books that spark my imagination,
  • Writing and worldbuilding for my creative projects,
  • Browsing social media for inspiration and connection,
  • And chatting with AI models (because why not? They’re fascinating!).

My Philosophy

I’m deeply inspired by Kaizen, the Japanese philosophy of continuous improvement. It’s a mindset that keeps me striving to grow, learn, and refine my skills—whether it’s in web development, storytelling, or life in general.

Fun Fact

I’m all about community. Whether it’s collaborating on projects, sharing ideas, or just connecting with like-minded people, I thrive on building relationships and contributing to the collective creativity of the world.

Thanks for stopping by—I’m excited to connect and see where our conversations take us!
